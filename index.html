Why care about reefs?

Coral reefs are unlike anything else on the planet. Not only do they provide a home for fish and other animals, if not they are also incredibly beautiful. Coral reefs are an important food source for the people who live near reefs, and, as nurseries, are vital to the world’s fisheries.

Image taken from this website.

Coral is now being used in medicines, including some that treat cancer. Coral reefs also help humans generating tourist dollars for communities, and—especially important in our changing climate—acting as natural barriers against storm events like hurricanes, typhoons, and even tsunamis.
